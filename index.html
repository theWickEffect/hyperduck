HyperDuck Lite

Game Audio Engine

HyperDuck Lite is a lightweight audio engine for game developement, offering a simple streamlined workflow and robust core functionality.

HDL is the perfect tool for game studios seeking simple way to integrate professional quallity scoring and sound effects.

Features:

  • Master mixing desk interface
  • Event triggered scoring and effects
  • Studio grade audio processing
  • Support for MP3 and WAV formats
  • Basic MIDI Functionality

HyperDuck Lite is entirely free and open source.
